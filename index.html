A Legacy of Quality Garment Making

For over three decades, Bhansu Apparel has been a trusted manufacturing partner for brands worldwide. Our journey began with a simple mission: to produce garments of uncompromising quality and fit.

We blend precision engineering with skilled craftsmanship. From pattern making and cutting to stitching and finishing, our team ensures every garment meets the highest standards of excellence.

Committed to a Greener Wardrobe

We believe in fashion that feels good and does good. Our sustainability efforts are stitched into every garment we produce.

Sustainable Fabrics

Sourcing certified organic cotton, recycled polyester, and other low-impact materials for our collections.

Waste Reduction

Optimizing our cutting processes to minimize fabric waste and recycling scraps whenever possible.

Ethical Factories

Ensuring fair wages, safe working conditions, and a commitment to the well-being of our entire production team.
